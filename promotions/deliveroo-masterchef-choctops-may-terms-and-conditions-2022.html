Promotion: Deliveroo MasterChef Promotion

Promoter: Deliveroo Australia Pty Ltd ABN 73 607 915 640, Level 2, 161 Collins St, Melbourne, VIC 3000, Australia. Ph: 1300 335 485

Start date: 09/06/22 (at approximately 8:30pm)

End date: 16/06/22 (while participating restaurants are open and while stocks last)

Eligible Entrants: This offer is only open to Australian residents. Individuals under the age of 18 must have parent or legal guardian approval to claim.

How to Claim: To claim a gift, the claimant must complete the following steps during the Promotional Period (while stocks last):

  1. spend at least $20 in one transaction (excluding service fees, tips and delivery fees) at a participating Deliveroo restaurant; and
  2. opt to receive the 'MasterChef inspired frozen treat' prior to finalising their order.  

Participating Deliveroo restaurants will include a reference to the offer on their order page (claimants can also access a list of participating restaurants on the Deliveroo website/app).  The MasterChef inspired frozen treat is strictly limited and only available while stocks last.  Participating restaurants will indicate on their order page (on the Deliveroo app) whether or not they have remaining stocks of the MasterChef inspired frozen treat.  

Participating restaurants will remove the option to claim the MasterChef inspired frozen treat once stocks have run out, however, there may be a short period of time the option to receive the MasterChef inspired frozen treat remains on the participating restaurant's page.  In this case, only the first 56 valid claims received will receive the MasterChef inspired frozen treat.  

All orders are subject to the Promoter's standard terms and conditions of service, available at deliveroo.com.au/legal.  The Promoter's standard geographical restrictions apply.

Claims permitted: There is a limit of one (1) claim per transaction (while stocks last).

Gift Description: The gift is a MasterChef inspired frozen treat (based on the episode of MasterChef aired in Melbourne on 09/06/22).  

Number of this gift: 56 per participating Deliveroo restaurant

Winning Method: Limited offer

Further Gift Details: The MasterChef inspired frozen treat may contain may contain traces of one or more of the following allergens or products: Wheat; Gluten; Peanuts; Nuts; Sesame Seeds; Milk; Eggs; Pork products; Mollusc; Crustaceans; Fish or Sulphur Dioxide.  The product is not Halal or suitable for vegetarians/vegans.  The full list of ingredients will be specified on the product packaging.  The Promoter strongly recommends that claimants read the product packaging prior to consuming the product.

The products are frozen and should not be left unrefrigerated for an extended period of time.  The Promoter recommends that the products are consumed upon delivery or otherwise placed in the freezer.
