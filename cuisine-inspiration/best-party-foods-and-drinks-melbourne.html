Throw A Party Melbourne Won’t Forget With These Awesome Party Foods And Drinks

  • 10 January 2018
  • Matt Lycett
  • Read time: 4 min
party mexican world food drinks melbourne finger food
Share this article

With the year just beginning, there are many things to celebrate in 2018. But throwing a party can be stressful. You have to be sure to get the best foods and drinks to cater to everyone's needs to achieve the title of 'The Perfect Host'. But fear not! We are here to help you find the best ingredients for a perfect party filled with yummy foods and tasty drinks to kick off the year in style, whether you're going for a Mexican fiesta or a high-end roaring '20s night.

1. Hang Over Pack - Hecho En Mexico

A post shared by Hecho en Mexico Brunswick (@hecho_en_mexico_brunswick) on Jul 25, 2017 at 5:44pm PDT

Help your guests fight that hangover before it has even begun by feeding them with the help of the Hang over Pack from Hecho En Mexico. This party pack has enough to fill everyone's tummies with a serving of Hecho's famous nachos, two of their tasty burritos and two of their Mexican Jarritos, a traditional Mexican soft drink. Once this is served, you'd better be quick as the food is so yummy it might not be around for long!

2. Grazing Platter - Crust

For party food your guests will remember, try out the Grazing Platter from Crust. This platter has so many tasty treats for your guests to enjoy, there is bound to be something for everyone. It is served with lamb ribs and mint yogurt, charred chorizo and coleslaw, tiger prawns glazed with delicious chilli jam, and a garnish of fresh chilli with rocket, herb and garlic strips. They are perfect to nibble on in between sips of wine and great conversation and are the perfect addition to any party.

3. Guacamole Del Senor - Si Senor

A post shared by Si Señor (@sisenorbalaclava) on Aug 25, 2017 at 10:37pm PDT

Mexican food is one of the best choices for any gathering and there is no better food for a party than a great big serving of guacamole and chips - and Si Senor has got you covered with their Guacamole Del Senor. They have taken their guacamole up a notch as it comes served with chorizo, delicious melted adobera cheese, fresh salsa, onion and coriander. Each bite is filled with fresh flavours and is so good you and your guests will find it hard to stop dipping in!

4. Medium Party Pack - Cellarbrations Flinders Street Liquor

A post shared by Cellarbrations Flinders St (@cellarbrationsflinders) on Dec 8, 2017 at 2:30am PST

If you and your guests want some booze to suit everyone's tastes without having to make a million stops, get it all in one order with the Medium Party Pack from Cellarbrations Flinders Street Liquor. Whether you are throwing an intimate dinner party or a big shindig, this party pack has it all. It comes with a bottle of Oyster Bay sauvignon blanc, one El Nino shiraz, Corona, Furphy, some small cups, party plates, Red Rock Deli Original, and Red Rock Deli sour cream chilli. You'll be hard-pressed to find any guests who aren't pleased with this, and if there are any, you probably don't want them there anyway!

There you have it, all the ingredients to throw a party that Melbourne will be talking about for years. So get your playlists ready, send out the invites and serve up a storm with these delicious party platters.
