Flippin’ Heck - Wake Up To Some Of The Best Pancakes In Sydney

  • 12 January 2018
  • Matt Lycett
  • Read time: 3 min
pancakes breakfast american european world foods foodies sydney
Share this article

Whether you like them as a sweet treat or a savoury delight, everybody loves pancakes. They are a great way to start the day or the perfect dessert to end a meal just right. Whether you have them the thick American way or the folded-over European way, they are always a great choice so let's look at some of the best pancakes in Sydney to inspire your next breakfast.

1. Ricotta Pancakes with Maple Bacon - Scrambled

A post shared by Dom Lotters (@dlotters) on Oct 10, 2015 at 7:40pm PDT

Forget about eggs and bacon, it's all about pancakes and bacon now and the Ricotta Pancakes with Maple Bacon served over at Scrambled are a treat to behold. These guys are known for their awesome breakfasts so don't miss out and be sure to start your day off sweet with this stack of fluffy pancakes served with a fresh berry compote, ice cream, and delicious maple bacon and you really can't go wrong.

2. The Pancakes - Eugene's

A post shared by By Nathan. (@collectionsfromhim) on May 28, 2015 at 4:43pm PDT

Another place serving awesome food is Eugene's. From breakfast right through to dessert, each meal is always on point and the Pancakes are no exception. These pancakes look so good you'll be forgiven for being that person who takes photos of their food. They don't just look good as they are fairly healthy too being made with organic buckwheat and buttermilk, and served with strawberry compote, cinnamon ricotta, and crushed pistachios; each bite is a total delight.

3. Poffertjes Dutch Pancakes - The Front Room

Take your taste buds on a Dutch adventure with these classic Poffertjes Pancakes served at The Front Room. This popular dish is inspired by the miniature puffy style of pancakes popular over in the Netherlands and they sure are tasty. These sweet treats are served with bananas, strawberries, and drizzled with maple syrup to make the perfect breakfast or dessert option to give you the taste of Europe without the bill at the end!

4. Peter's Pancakes - Sticky Fingers Kitchen and Bar

A post shared by Jarrett Galbreath (@jarrgal) on Dec 1, 2017 at 11:51am PST

For pancakes that have really been taken up a notch be sure to check out Peter's Pancakes served at Sticky Fingers Kitchen and Bar. These beauties have really set the bar high for all other pancakes as they are made with pandan, chocolate, syrup made with Thai milk tea, miso caramel ice cream and topped with seasonal fruits for a flavoursome meal you won't be disappointed by.

Whether you're a vegan, a meat-eater, or anything in between, no matter what your tastes, you'll be sure to find a pancake out there to suit you. These pancakes are some of the best in town, so get your pals together and place an order to try them out yourselves, and we promise you won't regret it!
