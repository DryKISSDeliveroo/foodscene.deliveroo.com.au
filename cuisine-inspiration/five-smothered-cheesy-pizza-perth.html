The big cheese - the best pizzas overflowing with cheese in Perth

  • 26 December 2017
  • by Lucy Thorpe
  • Read time: 3 min
perth pizza italian cheese mozzarella four cheese pizzeria
Share this article

Few of us can resist a pizza that is covered in fine toppings and smothered in glorious cheese. This is the type of food that can be eaten with great pleasure at any time of day and in any situation - cheese discrimination is not an option. If you want to find the top takeaway cheesy pizzas in Perth that will add a touch of fun to your day then you might like to consider the following Italian-style delights.

1. Meat Lovers Pizza - Café Zara

Eşsiz türk yemeklerinin tek adresi 🇹🇷

A post shared by Perth 58 Grose Avenue (@zara.cafe) on Apr 22, 2017 at 8:34pm PDT

All of the pizzas at Café Zara are family size, meaning that you get 15 inches of cheesy pizza pleasure every single time. They are all served with mixed veggies and cheese too. The selection covers Vegetarian, Salami, Meat Lovers, Chicken and Lamb. You will also find treats such as kebabs, chicken wings and Pide Turkish bread on the menu too.

2. Diavola Pizza - Threecoins Italian Trattoria

Excellent simple Italian dishes - family business for 3 generations #y_ltravel #ylonvacation #ylinperth #helloperth #seeperth #thisisWA #justanotherdayinwa

A post shared by Yvonne (@yvonne__lai) on Dec 3, 2017 at 2:33am PST

When you want to mix a great cheesy pizza with a spicy topping then ordering the Diavola Pizza at Threecoins Italian Trattoria is a smart move. This pizza is a mixture of tomato, mozzarella, spicy salami and fresh chilli. If you adore cheese and pizzas but aren't quite so keen on the fieriness of this pizza then you could opt for the likes of the Cicciona, the Capricciosa, the Romana or the Vegetariana instead.

3. Scugnizzo Pizza - Dough Pizza

@doughpizza know good pizza... proper napolitana style. 🍕 Its been a long time haunt of TSHN. The Parma comes with generous slices of prosciutto , rocket and a sprinkling of parmesan. With a strong Italian influence that reaches all the way from Naples to Perth, Dough Pizza is a place where you can enjoy some authentic pizzas with a rich tomato base. The choice of toppings here run from a classic Margherita to a mushroom-packed Funghi, a Regina and lots more options. If you want plenty of cheese then the Scugnizzo is a good choice. This has mozzarella, ventricina, smoked ham and gorgonzola all crammed onto it.

4. Ecco Special - Ecco Pizza

I ❤️ Rosemary & Potato Woodfired Pizza

A post shared by Spaghettihead - Louisa (@perthfoodgasmpics) on Aug 30, 2016 at 3:23am PDT

You get to choose from a wide range of Italian treats at the popular Ecco Pizza. This place has some tremendous salads and pasta on the menu but what if you really want a flavoursome pizza to light up your day? In that case, your first decision is whether to go for an olive oil base or a tomato sauce base. After that, there are plenty of different toppings to choose from. Many of your favourite pizza toppings are available here, while there are also some interesting flavour combinations that you might not have seen before. The Ecco Special gives you mozzarella, chicken, ham, salsiccia, capsicum and fresh herbs on a terrific tomato sauce base.

5. Submerged Prawns Pizza - Mondo Fresco

The pizza menu here at Mondo Fresco is enough to get your taste buds tingling once you start looking through it, with Pesto Presto, the New York Gangster and My Happy Wife all taking show-stopping places on the menu. For a wonderfully indulgent topping, the Submerged Prawns pizza is a great choice. This topping gives you slow cooked Napoli sauce, Spanish onions, mozzarella, prawns, kalamata olives, feta cheese, mushrooms, crushed chilli, parmesan and oregano.
