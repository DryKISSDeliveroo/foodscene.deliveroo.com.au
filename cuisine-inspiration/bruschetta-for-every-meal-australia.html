More than just a starter - why bruschetta is perfect for any meal, any time of day

  • 19 May 2018
  • Chloe Ryan
  • Read time: 3 min
italian
Share this article

Ah, bruschetta! Just the sound of those soft syllables is enough to make our mouths start watering. Tasty, flavoursome and delicious, this night-on-divine dish has too often been relegated to the role of starter.

No more! Versatile enough for every meal of the day, bruschetta is the perfect choice for breakfast, brunch, lunch. and dinner. And just in case you don't believe us, we're here and ready to prove it. If you're in need of some inspiration for your next Italian-style takeaway, just take a look at this lovely list of bruschetta-based goodness.

1. Indigo's Breakfast Bruschetta

A post shared by LUCY MACDONALD (@feeling.foodie) on Apr 3, 2018 at 4:07pm PDT

If you ever needed proof that bruschetta makes the perfect breakfast dish, then all you need to do is place an order with Indigo. Serving up the aptly titled Breakfast Bruschetta, they combine bacon, poached eggs, tomato, onion and avocado salsa with balsamic vinegar and basil. The perfect way to wake yourself up first thing, this delicious pick is packed full of scrumptious goodness that will leave you licking your lips for the rest of the day.

2. Cocoa and Rose's Avocado, Tomato and Feta Bruschetta

Whether you indulged in breakfast or missed the boat, there's always time to fit some brunch into your busy schedule. We have the bruschetta option that'll do the trick: Cocoa and Rose's Avocado, Tomato and Feta Bruschetta. Rolling beautifully off the tongue, it tastes as good as it sounds, and is finished with a mouth-wateringly yummy balsamic honey mustard sauce. This dish will fill that mid-morning space in your belly - it's the ideal light bite to see you through to a late lunch. Â Â
