New takes on the classic beef and ale pie

  • 1 June 2017
  • by Editor
  • Read time: 3 min
beef and ale pie pie beef
Share this article

We Aussies love the classic meat pie so much that we recently voted them the nation's favourite fast food – with an estimated 260 million put away by us each year. And with places like Katzy's, Piefection, Pure Pie and Tabet's Bakery, can you really blame us?

There's no limit to what you can put inside a pie. Here are four of our favourite twists on the beef and ale original.

Braised Beef, Guinness & Cheddar, Pure Pie

We'll start off simple. If you don't want to stray too far from the beaten path, you can't go wrong with Melbourne's own Pure Pie. We love the Braised Beef, Guinness & Cheddar for a proper hearty feast.

Tender beef is packed in with gooey cheddar cheese and a glug of Guinness for a taste of Ireland. Pie is literally the name of the game here, so you know you're guaranteed the real deal.

Where: Pure Pie, Melbourne

Jack Daniels BBQ Pork Ribs, Piefection

Our Famous Jack Daniels BBQ pork ribs topped with a slow cooked St.louis style BBQ pork rib bone in and covered in our Piefection BBQ sauce.

A post shared by Matto Roman (@piefectionpies) on Oct 18, 2016 at 7:01pm PDT

If you want some sass with your meat, nothing can beat Piefection's Jack Daniels BBQ Pork Ribs. This culinary creation is a pie topped with ribs. Now we think it's hard to beat this. Your classic pie is topped with slow-cooked St. Louis style barbecue ribs, smothered with Piefection's own secret BBQ sauce.

And the good news doesn't stop there – this delight comes in a family-sized form too, with a whole 1.3kg of pie-y goodness.

Where: Piefection, locations in Coorparoo and Mount Gravatt, Brisbane

Where: Katzy's, Sydney

Haloumi Pie, Tabet's Bakery

Mmm haloumi pie! #delish #brunswick #tabetsbakery #haloumiaddiction

A post shared by Pete Carseldine (@petepete83) on Dec 4, 2013 at 9:18pm PST

At Tabet's vegetarians can get in on the pie fun. This Lebanese bakery stuff most of their pies with cheese and vegetables, and cheesy pies are something we can get behind.

Choose from cheese and olive, cheese and vegetables and feta and spinach, along with the more exotic Shanklish pie, a Syrian and Lebanese blue cheese speciality. We especially love the Haloumi Pie for something different in your pastry case.

Where: Tabet's Bakery, Melbourne

Curry & Vegetable Pie, Katzy's

Before/after #meatpie #sydney #katzys

A post shared by Yoni Salomon (@the_traveling_physio) on Oct 6, 2016 at 8:10pm PDT

At Katzy's, you'll find all your classics, as well as some welcome unknowns. Though their Beef Pie knocks it out of the park every time, we love their Curry & Vegetable version too. This little beauty brings together all the good things about two different cuisines – bringing the spice from Indian and pie magic itself from British.

If you're a pie-junkie like us, you'll love the way this subtly spicy combination meets tender, flaky pastry for a real parcel of culinary fun.

Whether it's filo, short crust or puff, pastry is the one for us. Indulge in your pie cravings when you order through Deliveroo.
