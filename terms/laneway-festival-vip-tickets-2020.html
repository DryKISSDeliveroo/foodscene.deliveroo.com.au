  3. The total value of the Prize is $560.
  4. The Prize must be taken on the Date of the Laneway Festival as set out below, based on Location (subject to any change by event organisers):

Brisbane - 1/2/2020

Sydney - 2/2/2020

Adelaide - 7/2/2020

Melbourne - 8/2/2020

Fremantle - 9/2/2020
